<p><ul><li>Single Mode Fiber Pigtail</li><li>Multimode Fiber Pigtail</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fiber collimators are used to couple light from a fiber optic cable into a free-space optical system. The market for fiber collimators consists of two main types: single-mode fiber pigtail and multimode fiber pigtail. Single-mode fiber pigtail collimators are designed for use with single-mode fiber cables which have a small core size, allowing for better control and accuracy of the output beam. Multimode fiber pigtail collimators, on the other hand, are used with multimode fiber cables which have a larger core size, enabling the transmission of multiple light modes simultaneously.</p></p>
