<p><ul><li>Gasoline Direct Injection (GDI)</li><li>Port Fuel Injectors (PFI)</li><li>Diesel Fuel Injectors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fuel injectors are essential components in modern engines, with three main types dominating the market. Gasoline Direct Injection (GDI) injects fuel directly into the combustion chamber for improved fuel efficiency and power. Port Fuel Injectors (PFI) spray fuel into the intake port before it enters the combustion chamber, offering a more cost-effective solution. Diesel fuel injectors are specifically designed for diesel engines, delivering precise amounts of fuel into the combustion chamber for optimal performance and efficiency. Each type caters to different engine requirements and fuel types.</p></p>
