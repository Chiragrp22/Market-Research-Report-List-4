<p><p>Textile screen printing equipment can be categorized into three main types based on their level of automation - manual, semi-automatic, and fully automatic. Manual equipment requires operators to physically handle the screen printing process from start to finish. Semi-automatic equipment partly automates certain steps, such as screen exposure or printing, while requiring some manual intervention. Fully automatic equipment automates the entire screen printing process, including feeding, printing, and unloading, requiring minimal human intervention. These different types cater to varying production needs and levels of efficiency.</p></p>
