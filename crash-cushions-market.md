<p><p>Crash cushions are safety devices installed at the end of barriers or other road structures to absorb the impact of a vehicle collision. Non-redirective crash cushions are designed to absorb the impact energy without redirecting the vehicle, while redirective crash cushions are meant to redirect the vehicle away from the hazard. Both types of crash cushions serve the purpose of protecting drivers and passengers from serious injury in the event of a crash.</p></p>
