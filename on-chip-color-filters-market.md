<p><p>On-Chip Color Filters market includes various types such as Bayer Filter, RGBE Filter, RYYB Filter, and Others. Bayer filter is the most common type used in digital cameras, where each pixel is filtered with red, green, or blue. RGBE filter includes an additional emerald color for better image quality. RYYB filter uses red, yellow, and blue filters to improve low-light performance. Other types of filters can include different combinations of colors to achieve specific color reproduction goals in image sensors.</p></p>
