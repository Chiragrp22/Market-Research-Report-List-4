<p><p>Pharmacy Benefit Manager (PBM) market types can be classified into Type I and Type II. In Type I market, the PBM functions as an intermediary between pharmacies and payers, negotiating discounts and managing drug benefits. In contrast, Type II market involves PBMs owning their own pharmacies, creating conflicts of interest and potentially limiting competition. Both market types aim to streamline the prescription drug supply chain and control costs, but Type II can pose ethical concerns regarding transparency and fair competition.</p></p>
