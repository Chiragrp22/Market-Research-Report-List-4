<p><p>Veterinary Endoscopy Market consists of two main types: Flexible Endoscopy and Rigid Endoscopy. Flexible Endoscopy involves the use of a flexible tube with a camera and light at the tip for examining internal organs and tissues, providing better maneuverability in hard-to-reach areas. On the other hand, Rigid Endoscopy utilizes a straight and rigid tube with a camera and light for examining larger organs and cavities. Both types of endoscopy play a crucial role in diagnosing and treating various conditions in animals with precision and minimal invasiveness.</p></p>
