<p><strong>The Orthopedic Surgery Bone Cements Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Low Viscosity</li><li>Medium Viscosity</li><li>High Viscosity</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Orthopedic surgery bone cements are classified into three types based on their viscosity: low, medium, and high viscosity. Low viscosity bone cements are more fluid and easier to inject, making them suitable for vertebroplasty procedures. Medium viscosity bone cements offer a balance of flowability and strength, making them versatile for various orthopedic surgeries. High viscosity bone cements are thick and provide strong and stable fixation, ideal for load-bearing areas like the hip or knee joint. Each type caters to specific surgical needs and preferences.</p></p>
