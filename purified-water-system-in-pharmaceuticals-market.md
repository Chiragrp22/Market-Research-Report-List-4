<p><p>Purified water systems are essential in pharmaceuticals to ensure the safety and quality of products. Three common types of purified water systems in the market include Ion Exchange Systems, RO Systems, and Distillation Systems. Ion Exchange Systems use ion exchange resins to remove impurities, RO Systems utilize reverse osmosis to filter out contaminants, and Distillation Systems purify water through boiling and condensation. Each system has its own advantages and is chosen based on the specific needs and requirements of the pharmaceutical industry.</p></p>
