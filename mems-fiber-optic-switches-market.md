These switches are ideal for high-speed data transmission and telecommunications applications. On the other hand, MEMS Multimode Optical Switches are designed for use with multimode optical fibers, which have a larger core size and are typically used for shorter distance communication. These switches are ideal for applications that require high bandwidth and low-cost solutions.</p></p>
