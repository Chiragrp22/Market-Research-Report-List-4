<p><p>Worm geared motors come in two main types: helical-worm and double-worm. Helical-worm geared motors combine the characteristics of helical and worm gears, providing high efficiency and smooth operation. These motors are ideal for applications requiring high torque and low speed. On the other hand, double-worm geared motors feature two worm gears working together to multiply torque output. This design offers increased power transmission and precision, making them suitable for heavy-duty industrial applications. Both types of worm geared motors play crucial roles in various industries.</p></p>
