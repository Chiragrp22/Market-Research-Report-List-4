<p><p>Outdoor Warning Devices market includes three main types - Omni Directional, Rotating, and Directional. Omni Directional devices emit sound in all directions, providing wide coverage for large areas. Rotating devices use a rotating horn or siren to create a sweeping noise that can be heard over a larger area. Directional devices focus sound in a specific direction, ideal for targeting warnings to specific locations. Each type offers unique benefits depending on the size and layout of the area needing coverage.</p></p>
