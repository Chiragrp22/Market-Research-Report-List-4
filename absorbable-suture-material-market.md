<p><p>Absorbable suture material market is divided into two main types: synthetic material and natural material. Synthetic materials are typically derived from polymers like polyglycolic acid and polylactic acid, which are broken down and absorbed by the body over time. Natural materials, on the other hand, are sourced from animal or plant sources such as catgut or collagen. Both types of suture materials are designed to slowly dissolve in the body, eliminating the need for removal and reducing the risk of infection.</p></p>
