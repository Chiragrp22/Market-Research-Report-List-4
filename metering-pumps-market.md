<p><p>Metering pumps are precision pumps used in various industries for accurate dosing of chemicals or fluids. Diaphragm pumps, a type of metering pump, use a flexible diaphragm to displace the fluid. They are ideal for handling corrosive or viscous fluids. Piston/plunger pumps, another type, use a reciprocating piston or plunger to displace the fluid. They are known for their high pressure capabilities and are commonly used in oil and gas, chemical, and water treatment industries. Both types of pumps play a crucial role in ensuring precise and reliable fluid dosing.</p></p>
