Peristaltic Tube Pumps utilize a rotating rotor to compress and expand a flexible tube, while Peristaltic Hose Pumps use a similar mechanism with a hose instead of a tube. Both types offer precise and accurate fluid transfer in industrial applications, making them ideal for industries such as pharmaceuticals, chemical processing, and food and beverage production. These pumps are known for their reliability, efficiency, and ease of maintenance.</p></p>
