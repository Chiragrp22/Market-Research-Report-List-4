<p><ul><li>Electromagnetic Induction</li><li>Magnetic Resonance</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive inductive wireless charging systems use electromagnetic induction or magnetic resonance technology to transfer power wirelessly from a charging pad to a vehicle's battery. Electromagnetic induction works by creating a magnetic field between the charging pad and the vehicle, inducing an electric current in the receiver coil. Magnetic resonance technology allows for greater distance between the charging pad and the vehicle while maintaining efficient power transfer. Both types of systems are popular in the automotive industry for their convenience and ease of use.</p></p>
