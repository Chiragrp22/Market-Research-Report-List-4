<p><p>Semiconductor Dry Etching System is a type of semiconductor etching system that uses plasma to remove material from a semiconductor wafer. It is a precise and controlled process commonly used in the manufacturing of integrated circuits. On the other hand, Semiconductor Wet Etching System is a method that uses liquid chemicals to remove material from a semiconductor wafer. It is a more traditional etching method but is still widely used in certain applications for its simplicity and cost-effectiveness.</p></p>
