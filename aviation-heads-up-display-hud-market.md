<p><p>The Aviation Heads-up Display (HUD) market consists of two main types: Conventional HUD and Augmented Reality (AR)-Based HUD. Conventional HUDs display vital flight information directly in the pilot's line of sight, improving situational awareness and reducing the need to look down at instruments. On the other hand, AR-Based HUDs overlay digital information onto the real world, enhancing the pilot's view with additional data and graphics. Both types aim to improve pilot performance, safety, and decision-making during flight.</p></p>
