<p><p>A spray dryer is a device used to transform liquid or slurry feed into a dried powder by spraying the feed into a hot medium such as air. The spray drying process involves atomizing the feed into small droplets and then rapidly drying these droplets to form a powder. This method is commonly used in the food, pharmaceutical, chemical, and other industries to produce powders with specific particle sizes, shapes, and properties.</p><p>The Spray Dryer Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 5.70% during the forecast period.
