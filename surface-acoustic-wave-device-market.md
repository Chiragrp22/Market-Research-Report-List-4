<p><p>Surface acoustic wave devices are used in various applications such as filters, oscillators, resonators, transducers, and others. Filters are used to selectively pass or reject certain frequencies, while oscillators generate oscillating signals. Resonators are used to maintain vibrations at a particular frequency, and transducers convert energy from one form to another. Other types of surface acoustic wave devices include sensors, delay lines, and modulators. Each type serves a unique purpose in different electronic systems and industries.</p></p>
