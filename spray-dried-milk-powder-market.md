<p><p>Spray dried milk powder market is divided into two main types: Full Cream Milk Powder and Skimmed Milk Powder. Full Cream Milk Powder is made from whole cow's milk and contains higher fat content, making it creamier and richer in taste. On the other hand, Skimmed Milk Powder is made by removing the fat from whole milk, resulting in a product with lower fat content. Both types of milk powder have different applications in the food industry and cater to different consumer preferences.</p></p>
