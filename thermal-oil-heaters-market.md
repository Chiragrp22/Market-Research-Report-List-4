<p><ul><li>Tubular Heating</li><li>Local Rapid Heating</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Thermal oil heaters can be categorized into two main types: tubular heating and local rapid heating. Tubular heating systems use a network of tubes to transfer heat to the thermal oil, providing consistent and uniform heating throughout the system. On the other hand, local rapid heating systems target specific areas or components that require heating, allowing for quick and targeted temperature control. Both types of thermal oil heaters are commonly used in various industries such as oil and gas, chemical, and food processing.</p></p>
