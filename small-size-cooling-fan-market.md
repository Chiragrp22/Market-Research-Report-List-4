<p><p>The small size cooling fan market can be classified into three main types: Axial Fans, Centrifugal Fans, and Others. Axial fans are designed to move air parallel to the fan's axis, making them ideal for applications requiring high airflow. Centrifugal fans, on the other hand, use rotating impellers to increase the speed and pressure of the air, making them suitable for applications requiring higher pressure. The remaining category, Others, includes various types of cooling fans that do not fall into the axial or centrifugal fan categories.</p></p>
