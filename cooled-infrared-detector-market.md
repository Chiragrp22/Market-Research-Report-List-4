<p><p>Cooled infrared detector market consists of various types, including photoconductive detector (HgCdTe), photovoltaic detector, Schottky barrier detector, quantum well detector, and other types. Photoconductive detectors use HgCdTe materials to detect infrared radiation, while photovoltaic detectors convert infrared radiation directly into electrical signals. Schottky barrier detectors utilize metal-semiconductor junctions, and quantum well detectors use quantum confinement effects to detect infrared radiation. Other types of detectors include bolometers, pyroelectric detectors, and microbolometers, each with unique characteristics and applications in infrared sensing technology.</p></p>
