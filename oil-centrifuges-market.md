<p><ul><li>Swinging-bucket Rotors</li><li>Fixed-angle Rotors</li><li>Vertical Rotors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Oil centrifuges are classified into different types based on the design of the rotor. Swinging-bucket rotors have buckets that swing outward during acceleration, providing a horizontal plane for separation. Fixed-angle rotors have tubes that remain at a fixed angle during the centrifugation process. Vertical rotors have tubes that move along a vertical axis, allowing for efficient separation of oil from other components. Each type of rotor is suited for specific applications and industries within the oil centrifuges market.</p></p>
