<p><p>Wave power generation equipment market includes various types of equipment such as direct mechanical transmission, hydraulic transmission, and pneumatic actuators. Direct mechanical transmission systems use gears and levers to convert wave energy into mechanical power. Hydraulic transmission systems use fluids to transfer energy from the waves to a hydraulic motor. Pneumatic actuators use air pressure to convert wave energy into motion. Each type of equipment has its advantages and limitations in terms of efficiency, cost, and maintenance requirements.</p></p>
