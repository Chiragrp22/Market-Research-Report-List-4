<p><strong>The Helicopter Autopilot Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Attitude Hold</li><li>Coupled</li><li>SAS</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Helicopter autopilot systems are categorized into different types based on their functions and capabilities. Attitude hold systems maintain a desired orientation in the air, while coupled systems automatically control both attitude and navigation functions. Stability augmentation systems (SAS) enhance a helicopter's stability and control during flight. Other types of autopilot systems may offer additional features such as altitude hold, heading hold, or GPS navigation capabilities. Each type of autopilot system serves a specific purpose in improving flight safety and efficiency for helicopter operations.</p></p>
