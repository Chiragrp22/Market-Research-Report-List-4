<p><ul><li>Single Channel</li><li>Dual Channels</li></ul></p>
<p><p>TENS machines are available in two main market types: single channel and dual channels. Single channel TENS machines have one set of electrodes, making them suitable for targeting one area of the body at a time. On the other hand, dual channel TENS machines have two sets of electrodes, allowing for the simultaneous treatment of two areas or different settings for intensity. Both types are popular choices for managing pain and promoting muscle relaxation, with dual channel machines offering more versatility in treatment options.</p></p>
