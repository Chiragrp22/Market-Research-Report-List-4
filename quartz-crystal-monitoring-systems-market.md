On the other hand, liquid applications involve monitoring processes that occur in a liquid medium, such as chemical reactions or biological assays. Both types of monitoring systems utilize quartz crystals to accurately measure and monitor various parameters to ensure the smooth operation of the processes they are monitoring.</p></p>
