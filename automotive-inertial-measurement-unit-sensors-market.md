<p><strong>The Automotive Inertial Measurement Unit Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>MEMS gyroscope-based IMUs</li><li>FOG-based IMUs</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive Inertial Measurement Unit (IMU) sensors are categorized into two main types: MEMS gyroscope-based IMUs and FOG-based IMUs. MEMS gyroscope-based IMUs are more commonly used due to their smaller size, lower cost, and better power efficiency. They are suitable for applications that require accurate measurement of angular velocity. On the other hand, FOG-based IMUs offer higher accuracy and stability but are larger and more expensive. They are typically used in high-end automotive systems that demand superior precision.</p></p>
