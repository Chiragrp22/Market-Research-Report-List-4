<p><p>Smart Vision Camera Market types include Line Scan Camera and Area Scan Camera. Line Scan Cameras are used for capturing moving objects, as they produce a continuous image by scanning the object line by line. They are ideal for inspecting large objects or surfaces. Area Scan Cameras, on the other hand, capture a complete image at once without the need for scanning. They are suitable for inspecting stationary objects or smaller areas with high resolution. Both types of cameras have their own advantages and are used in various industrial applications.</p></p>
