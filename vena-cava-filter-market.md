<p><p>Vena Cava Filters are medical devices designed to prevent blood clots from travelling to the lungs and causing pulmonary embolism. There are two main types of Vena Cava Filters: Inferior Vena Cava Filters and Superior Vena Cava Filters. Inferior Vena Cava Filters are placed in the inferior vena cava, which is the large vein that carries deoxygenated blood from the lower body to the heart. Superior Vena Cava Filters are placed in the superior vena cava, which carries deoxygenated blood from the upper body to the heart.</p></p>
