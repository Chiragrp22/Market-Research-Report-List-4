<p><p>Tyre shredders come in two main types: hydraulic and electric. Hydraulic tyre shredders are powered by hydraulic systems, which use fluid pressure to crush and shred tires. They are known for their high power and efficiency. On the other hand, electric tyre shredders rely on an electric motor for operation. They are environmentally friendly and more suitable for indoor use due to their lack of emissions. Both types of tyre shredders are essential for recycling old tires and reducing waste.</p></p>
