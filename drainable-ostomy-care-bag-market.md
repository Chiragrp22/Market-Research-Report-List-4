<p><p>Drainable ostomy care bags are available in three main types: one-piece systems, two-piece systems, and skin barriers. One-piece systems consist of a bag attached directly to the skin barrier, which is applied directly to the stoma. Two-piece systems feature a separate skin barrier that attaches to the stoma, and a separate bag that can be removed and replaced as needed. Skin barriers are specially designed to protect the skin around the stoma from irritation and leakage. Each type offers unique benefits for individuals managing ostomy care needs.</p></p>
