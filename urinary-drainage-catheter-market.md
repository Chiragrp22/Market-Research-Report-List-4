<p><p>Urinary drainage catheters are medical devices used to drain urine from the bladder. They are generally divided into three main types based on the material they are made of: rubber, plastic (PVC), and silicone. Rubber catheters are flexible and soft, but may cause allergic reactions. Plastic catheters, specifically those made of PVC, are more rigid and commonly used. Silicone catheters are soft, biocompatible, and flexible, making them suitable for long-term use. Each type has its own advantages and considerations for specific medical needs.</p></p>
