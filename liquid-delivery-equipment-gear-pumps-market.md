<p><ul><li>External Gear Pump</li><li>Internal Gear Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Gear pumps are a type of liquid delivery equipment commonly used in various industries. There are two main types of gear pumps: external gear pumps and internal gear pumps. External gear pumps have two external gears that rotate in opposite directions, creating suction and pushing the liquid through the pump. Internal gear pumps have an external gear rotating around an internal gear, creating a pumping action. Both types are efficient at delivering liquids and are widely used in applications requiring precise and consistent flow rates.</p></p>
