<p><p>The wireless tower market consists of three main types: three tube wireless towers, angle-steel wireless towers, and guyed wireless towers. Three tube wireless towers are made up of three vertical tubes connected by horizontal supports, providing stability and strength. Angle-steel wireless towers are constructed using angled steel sections, offering cost-effective solutions for medium to large communication projects. Guyed wireless towers are supported by guy wires connected to the ground, providing extra support and height for larger coverage areas. Each type of tower serves different purposes based on project requirements.</p></p>
