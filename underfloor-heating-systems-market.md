<p><p>Underfloor Heating Systems can be classified into two main types: Gas Heating and Electric Heating. Gas heating systems utilize a boiler to heat water which is then distributed through pipes under the floor, providing consistent and efficient heating. On the other hand, Electric heating systems utilize electric cables or heating mats under the floor to generate warmth. Both types offer energy-efficient and comfortable heating solutions for residential and commercial spaces, with Electric Heating being more popular due to its lower installation costs and ease of use.</p></p>
