<p><p>Transformer bushings are essential components in electrical transformers, providing insulation and support for conductor connections. There are three main types of transformer bushings: Solid Type, Resin Impregnated Paper, and Oil Impregnated Paper. Solid Type bushings are made of solid insulation materials, while Resin Impregnated Paper bushings have paper insulation impregnated with resin for enhanced insulation properties. Each type of bushing has its own set of advantages and applications in the transformer market.</p></p>
