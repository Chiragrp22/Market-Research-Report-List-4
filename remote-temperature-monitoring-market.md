<p><p>Remote Temperature Monitoring Market includes two types of sensors: Non-Contact Temperature Sensors and Contact Temperature Sensors. </p><p>Non-Contact Temperature Sensors use infrared technology to measure temperature from a distance, making them ideal for monitoring temperatures in hard-to-reach or hazardous environments. </p><p>Contact Temperature Sensors, on the other hand, require physical contact with the surface to measure temperature accurately. They are commonly used in industries where precise temperature control is necessary. Both types of sensors play a crucial role in various applications such as healthcare, industrial, and environmental monitoring.</p></p>
