<p><ul><li>Open-Loop Piezo Controllers</li><li>Closed-Loop Piezo Controllers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Piezo Controllers are devices used to control the movement of piezoelectric actuators, which are used in various applications such as nano-positioning, microscopy, and precision machining. There are two main types of Piezo Controllers: Open-Loop and Closed-Loop. </p><p>Open-Loop Piezo Controllers provide a basic voltage signal to drive the actuator without feedback, making them simpler and more cost-effective but less accurate. </p><p>Closed-Loop Piezo Controllers use feedback from sensors to continuously adjust the signal, providing higher precision and accuracy but at a higher cost.</p></p>
