<p><p>Transradial access products market includes catheters, guidewires, sheaths, and sheath introducers. Catheters are used to access blood vessels and deliver medications or perform procedures. Guidewires help navigate through blood vessels during procedures. Sheaths are used to maintain access to the vessel and introduce catheters or other devices. Sheath introducers provide a smooth entry point for larger devices like sheaths. These products are essential for minimally invasive procedures through the radial artery, offering advantages such as decreased bleeding and faster recovery times.</p></p>
