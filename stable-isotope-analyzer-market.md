<p><p>Stable Isotope Analyzer Market includes two main types: Stable IRMS and Laser-based. Stable IRMS (Isotope Ratio Mass Spectrometry) technology analyses isotope ratios by separating ions based on their mass-to-charge ratio. It is widely used in various scientific fields such as environmental research, geology, and food authenticity testing. On the other hand, Laser-based analyzers utilize laser spectrometry to measure isotopic ratios. This technology offers rapid and accurate analysis of isotopes, making it desirable for research and industry applications requiring precise measurements.</p></p>
