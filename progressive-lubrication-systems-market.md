<p><p>Progressive lubrication systems market includes manual and electric types. Manual systems rely on manual operation for dispensing lubricant to various points in machinery, making them more suitable for smaller-scale applications. Electric systems, on the other hand, automate the lubrication process using electric pumps and controllers, making them more efficient for larger industrial applications. Both types offer benefits such as improved maintenance, reduced downtime, and extended equipment life, catering to different needs in the market.</p></p>
