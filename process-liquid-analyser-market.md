<p><p>Process Liquid Analyser market consists of various types such as NIR Based, NMR Based, ECD Sensor Based, and Laser Based analyzers. NIR Based analyzers utilize near-infrared light to analyze liquid samples, while NMR Based analyzers use nuclear magnetic resonance technology. ECD Sensor Based analyzers rely on electrochemical sensors for analysis, and Laser Based analyzers use laser technology for accurate measurements. Each type has its own unique capabilities and applications in process industries for monitoring and controlling liquid processes.</p></p>
