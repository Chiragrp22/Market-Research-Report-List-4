<p><ul><li>Dark Field Inspection</li><li>Bright Field Inspection</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Optical Semiconductor Inspection Equipment market encompasses different types of inspection techniques like Dark Field Inspection and Bright Field Inspection. Dark Field Inspection involves illuminating the sample with oblique light, enabling detection of defects based on the scattered light. On the other hand, Bright Field Inspection involves illuminating the sample from above and detecting defects based on the reflected light. Both techniques are widely used in the semiconductor industry for detecting imperfections and ensuring product quality.</p></p>
