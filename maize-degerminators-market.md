<p><p>Maize degerminators are machines used to separate the germ from the maize kernel. There are two main types of maize degerminators: horizontal type and vertical type. Horizontal type degerminators are designed with a horizontal axis for the maize to pass through the machine, while vertical type degerminators use a vertical axis for the maize to be processed. Both types offer efficient separation of the germ from the maize kernel, but the choice between horizontal and vertical types depends on the specific needs and preferences of the user.</p></p>
