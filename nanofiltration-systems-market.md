<p><p>Nanofiltration systems are a type of water treatment technology that can be categorized into two main types: spiral membrane and tubular membrane systems. Spiral membrane systems use a spiral-wound design that allows for high efficiency and compactness, making them suitable for residential and small-scale applications. On the other hand, tubular membrane systems use a series of tubes for filtration, making them more suitable for larger industrial applications due to their higher capacity and scalability. Both systems are effective in removing contaminants and producing clean water.</p></p>
