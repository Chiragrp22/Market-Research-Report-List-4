<p><p>Narrow Belt Sorters are commonly available in three different market types: 30°, 45°, and 90°. These market types refer to the angles at which the diverting arms are positioned to push items off the sorter conveyor. In a 30° market, items are diverted at a slight angle, while in a 45° market, items are pushed off at a sharper angle. The 90° market type involves diverting items at a right angle. Each market type offers unique advantages and is chosen based on the specific requirements of the sorting process.</p></p>
