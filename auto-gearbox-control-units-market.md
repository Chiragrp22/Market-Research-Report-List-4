<p><ul><li>Semi-automatic</li><li>Fully Automatic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Auto Gearbox Control Units in the market are available in two main types: Semi-automatic and Fully Automatic. Semi-automatic control units allow the driver to manually shift gears using paddle shifters or a gear lever, while also having the option for the gearbox to automatically shift gears when needed. Fully automatic control units, on the other hand, take full control of shifting gears without any input from the driver. Both types offer convenience and ease of use, with fully automatic units being more hands-free and easy for the driver.</p></p>
