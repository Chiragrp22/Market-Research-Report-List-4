<p><p>Dry laminating machines are classified into two types- Coating dry laminating machines and pre-coated dry laminating machines. Coating dry laminating machines are used to apply a layer of adhesive to the substrate before laminating, while pre-coated dry laminating machines are used to laminate substrates already coated with adhesive. Both types of machines are essential for producing high-quality laminated products such as packaging materials, labels, and other industrial applications. The choice between the two types depends on the specific requirements of the laminating process.</p></p>
