<p><p>Aircraft tilt sensors market includes two main types - Analog Type and Digital Type. Analog Type sensors provide continuous output values proportional to the tilt angle, offering precise measurements but requiring additional processing for data interpretation. On the other hand, Digital Type sensors provide digital output signals in binary code, offering simple data processing but may be less precise than analog sensors. Both types have their own advantages and are utilized in various applications within the aviation industry.</p></p>
