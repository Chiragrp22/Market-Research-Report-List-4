<p><p>Blockchain in healthcare market can be categorized into public, private, and permissioned blockchains. Public blockchains are decentralized and open to anyone, providing transparency and security for all participants. Private blockchains are controlled by a single organization, limiting access to authorized users and focusing on confidentiality. Permissioned blockchains allow selected participants to have control over who can access and participate in the network, ensuring trust and accountability within a specified group. Each type offers unique benefits and use cases in the healthcare industry.</p></p>
