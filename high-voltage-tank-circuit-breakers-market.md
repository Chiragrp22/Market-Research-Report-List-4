Dead Tank Circuit Breakers are mounted on a metal frame outside of the tank filled with insulating gas, making them suitable for harsh environments. Live Tank Circuit Breakers are mounted directly in the tank filled with insulating gas, allowing for more compact design and easier maintenance. Both types are crucial for interrupting high voltage circuits safely and efficiently.</p></p>
