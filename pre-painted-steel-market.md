<p><ul><li>Hot Dip Galvanised Steel (HDG)</li><li>Galvanized Mild Steel (GMS)</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pre-Painted Steel Market Types can be classified into three main categories: Hot Dip Galvanised Steel (HDG), Galvanized Mild Steel (GMS), and Others. HDG involves coating steel with a layer of zinc through a hot dip process, offering high corrosion resistance. GMS, on the other hand, refers to a similar process but with a thinner coating, making it suitable for less harsh environments. The category of Others includes various types of pre-painted steel with different coating materials and processes.</p></p>
