<p><ul><li>EUV</li><li>Non EUV</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Reticle POD market can be divided into two main types: EUV and Non-EUV. EUV (Extreme Ultraviolet) reticle PODs are designed for use with EUV lithography systems and offer advanced features to support the high precision requirements of these systems. Non-EUV reticle PODs, on the other hand, are used with traditional optical lithography systems, and are designed to meet the unique needs of these systems. Both types of reticle PODs play a crucial role in the semiconductor industry by ensuring the precise transfer of patterns onto silicon wafers.</p></p>
