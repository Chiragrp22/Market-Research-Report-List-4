<p><p>UAV GNSS antennas come in two main types: helix antennas and patch antennas. Helix antennas are known for their high efficiency and wide beamwidth, making them ideal for applications where consistent signal reception is critical. Patch antennas are compact and lightweight, making them suitable for smaller UAVs where space is limited. Both types offer accurate and reliable positioning data for unmanned aerial vehicles, ensuring smooth navigation and operation in various environments.</p></p>
