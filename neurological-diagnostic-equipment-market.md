<p><p>Neurological diagnostic equipment market can be divided into two main types: internal and external. Internal equipment includes devices that are directly inserted or used inside the body to measure brain activity or diagnose neurological conditions, such as EEG electrodes or intracranial pressure monitors. External equipment, on the other hand, includes devices that are used externally on the body to conduct tests or scans, such as MRI machines or CT scanners. Both types play a crucial role in the diagnosis and treatment of neurological disorders.</p></p>
