<p><strong>The Uncooled Infrared Thermal Camera Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Vanadium Oxide (VOx)</li><li>Amorphous Silicon (A-Si)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Uncooled infrared thermal cameras are categorized into two main types: Vanadium Oxide (VOx) and Amorphous Silicon (A-Si). VOx-based cameras offer higher sensitivity and image quality, making them ideal for high-end applications such as security and surveillance. On the other hand, A-Si cameras are more cost-effective and widely used in consumer electronics and automotive systems. Both types of cameras are becoming increasingly popular due to their compact size, low power consumption, and ability to detect heat signatures without the need for cooling.</p></p>
