<p><p>Military laser systems are classified into different types based on their technology. Fiber lasers use optical fibers as the gain medium, providing high power and efficiency. Chemical lasers utilize chemical reactions to generate laser beams for long-range applications. CO2 lasers use carbon dioxide gas as the gain medium, producing high-power infrared beams. Semiconductor lasers are compact and energy efficient, suitable for portable and lightweight military applications. Each type offers unique capabilities and advantages for various military operations.</p></p>
