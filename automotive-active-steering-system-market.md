<p><ul><li>Active Front Axle Steering</li><li>Active Rear Axle Steering</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automotive Active Steering System market includes two main types: Active Front Axle Steering and Active Rear Axle Steering. Active Front Axle Steering involves the steering of both front wheels to enhance stability, control, and responsiveness while driving. Active Rear Axle Steering focuses on steering the rear wheels in the opposite direction of the front wheels at low speeds for improved maneuverability and in the same direction at higher speeds for better handling and stability. Both systems aim to enhance overall driving performance and safety.</p></p>
