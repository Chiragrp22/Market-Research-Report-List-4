<p><ul><li>Inkjet</li><li>Gravure</li><li>Screen</li><li>Flexography</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Printed electronics products market can be classified based on the printing technology used, including inkjet, gravure, screen, flexography, and others. Inkjet printing technology involves precision printing using droplets of ink, while gravure utilizes an engraved cylinder to transfer ink. Screen printing uses a mesh screen to apply ink onto surfaces, and flexography involves a flexible relief plate. The "others" category encompasses various other printing technologies that are used in the production of printed electronics products.</p></p>
