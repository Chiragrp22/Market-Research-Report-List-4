<p><p>A dental air turbine is a type of handpiece used in dentistry to power rotary cutting instruments such as burs and drills. Dental air turbines are essential tools in dental practices for procedures such as cavity preparation, tooth restoration, and crown placement.</p><p>The Dental Air Turbine Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 5.2% during the forecast period. This growth can be attributed to factors such as the increasing prevalence of dental diseases and the rising demand for cosmetic dentistry procedures. Technological advancements in dental air turbines, such as improved ergonomics, reduced noise levels, and better precision, are also driving market growth.</p><p>One of the latest trends in the Dental Air Turbine Market is the increasing adoption of electric handpieces over traditional air turbines. Electric handpieces offer benefits such as higher torque, greater control, and quieter operation, which are appealing to dental professionals. <p><ul><li>Inside Exhaust</li><li>Outside Exhaust</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Dental air turbines can be classified into two main types: Inside Exhaust and Outside Exhaust. Inside Exhaust turbines have the exhaust air expelled into the patient's mouth, allowing for a smaller and lighter handpiece design. On the other hand, Outside Exhaust turbines release the exhaust air outside of the handpiece, preventing it from entering the patient's mouth. Both types have their own advantages and drawbacks, catering to the varying preferences and needs of dental professionals.</p></p>
