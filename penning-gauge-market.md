<p><p>Penning Gauge market includes two main types: Ordinary Penning Gauge and Magnetron Penning Gauge. The Ordinary Penning Gauge measures pressure by ionizing gas particles through a filament discharge. It is commonly used for rough vacuum measurements. On the other hand, the Magnetron Penning Gauge utilizes a magnetic field to enhance the ionization process, providing more accurate pressure readings in the high vacuum range. Both types play important roles in various industries such as semiconductor manufacturing, aerospace, and research laboratories.</p></p>
