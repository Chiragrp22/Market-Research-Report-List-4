<p><p>Small Modular Reactors (SMRs) are a type of nuclear reactor that are smaller in size and can be easily transported and assembled on-site. They are being developed in two main market types: Thermal-neutron Reactors and Fast-neutron Reactors. Thermal-neutron reactors use slow-moving neutrons to sustain nuclear fission reactions, while fast-neutron reactors use high-energy neutrons to trigger fission. Both types have unique advantages, such as increased safety, efficiency, and flexibility, making them attractive options for the future of nuclear energy.</p></p>
