<p><p>High Temperature Sensors market consists of various types including Resistance Temperature Detectors (RTDs), Thermocouples, and Semiconductor-based Sensors. RTDs measure temperature by changes in electrical resistance, while Thermocouples generate voltage based on temperature differences. Semiconductor-based sensors use semiconductor materials to measure temperature. Each type has its own advantages and applications, with RTDs being highly accurate, Thermocouples being versatile and durable, and Semiconductor-based sensors offering fast response times. Overall, these sensors cater to different temperature measurement needs across a wide range of industries.</p></p>
