<p><p>Hydrolyzed infant formula comes in two main types - completely hydrolyzed and partially hydrolyzed. Completely hydrolyzed formula is broken down into smaller proteins, making it easier for babies with severe allergies or intolerance to digest. Partially hydrolyzed formula has larger protein molecules that are broken down to some extent, offering a middle ground for infants with mild to moderate sensitivities. Both types aim to provide a suitable alternative for infants who cannot tolerate standard formulas due to cow's milk protein allergy or other digestive issues.</p></p>
