<p><p>Radiation processing in the food industry is carried out using two main types of technology, gamma-based and accelerator-based. Gamma-based processing involves the use of gamma rays emitted from a radioactive source to eliminate harmful bacteria and insects in food products. On the other hand, accelerator-based processing uses high-energy electrons to achieve the same results. Both methods are effective in extending the shelf life of food products, improving safety, and maintaining quality without the need for chemicals or preservatives.</p></p>
