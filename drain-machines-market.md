<p><p>Drain machines are primarily categorized into three main types: sewer jetters, cable machines, and others. Sewer jetters use high-pressure water to clear blockages in drains and pipes. Cable machines, on the other hand, utilize rotating cables with cutting blades to break through clogs. The "others" category may include specialized drain machines such as drum machines and root cutters. Each type of drain machine offers unique benefits and capabilities for effectively addressing different types of drain and sewer clogs.</p></p>
