<p><p>Heavy duty conveyor belts are classified into three main types: multiply fabric conveyor belts, steel cord conveyor belts, and solid woven conveyor belts. Multiply fabric conveyor belts are made from multiple layers of fabric for strength and durability. Steel cord conveyor belts use steel cords for reinforcement, making them ideal for carrying heavy loads over long distances. Solid woven conveyor belts are made from a single ply of fabric for rugged applications. Each type of conveyor belt has its own unique characteristics and is designed for specific industrial applications.</p></p>
