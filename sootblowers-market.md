<p><p>Sootblowers are used in power plants to remove soot and ash buildup on boiler tubes. There are three main types of sootblowers: retractable, rotative, and others. Retractable sootblowers are mounted on a retractable lance that can extend into the boiler for cleaning. Rotative sootblowers have rotating nozzles that spray water or steam onto the tubes. Other types of sootblowers include sonic or sonic horn sootblowers, which use sound waves to dislodge deposits. Each type of sootblower has its own advantages and is used in different applications based on the specific needs of the boiler.</p></p>
