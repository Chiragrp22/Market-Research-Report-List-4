<p><p>Liquid level transducers are used to measure and monitor the level of liquids in various applications. There are two main types of liquid level transducers in the market: contact and non-contact. Contact transducers make direct contact with the liquid to measure its level, while non-contact transducers use technologies such as ultrasonic or radar to measure the level without physically touching the liquid. Contact transducers are typically more cost-effective, while non-contact transducers are preferred for applications where avoiding contact with the liquid is necessary.</p></p>
