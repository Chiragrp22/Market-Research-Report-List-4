<p><ul><li>Manifold Lyophilization Equipment</li><li>Rotary Lyophilization Equipment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Manifold Lyophilization Equipment is a type of lyophilization equipment that consists of multiple ports or connections where several samples can be freeze-dried simultaneously. Rotary Lyophilization Equipment, on the other hand, operates by placing the samples in a rotating drum, providing uniform drying and efficient lyophilization. Both types of equipment are commonly used in laboratories and pharmaceutical industries for freeze-drying various substances such as pharmaceuticals, food products, and biological samples.</p></p>
