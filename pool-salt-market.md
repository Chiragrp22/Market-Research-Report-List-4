<p><ul><li>Rock Salt</li><li>Solar Salt</li><li>Evap Salt</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pool salt market consists of three main types: rock salt, solar salt, and evap salt. Rock salt is mined from underground salt deposits and is the most affordable option. Solar salt is created through solar evaporation of seawater and is known for its high purity. Evap salt is produced through solution mining of underground salt deposits and is known for its fast dissolving properties. Each type offers its own unique benefits and cater to different preferences of pool owners.</p></p>
