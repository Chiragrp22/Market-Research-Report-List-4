<p><p>Couplant heaters are available in two main types: single cup and double cup. Single cup couplant heaters have one cup for holding the couplant material, while double cup couplant heaters have two cups. Single cup heaters are easier to use and more compact, making them suitable for smaller applications. On the other hand, double cup heaters allow for heating multiple couplant materials simultaneously, making them more convenient for larger scale operations. Both types offer efficient and reliable heating solutions for ensuring proper ultrasonic testing procedures.</p></p>
