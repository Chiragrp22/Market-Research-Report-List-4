<p><p>Sterile surgical catgut is a type of suture material used in surgical procedures for wound closure. Catgut is made from natural collagen fibers derived from the submucosa of the small intestines of sheep or goats. It is absorbable and gradually dissolves in the body. Chromic catgut is a type of catgut that has been treated with chromic salts to delay absorption and increase its tensile strength. Both types of catgut are widely used in various surgical procedures due to their effectiveness and biocompatibility.</p></p>
