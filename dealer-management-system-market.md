<p><p>Dealer Management System (DMS) market can be categorized into two types: On-Premise and Cloud-based. On-Premise DMS requires the software to be installed and managed directly on the dealer's servers, allowing for full control but requiring more maintenance. Cloud-based DMS, on the other hand, is hosted on remote servers and accessed over the internet, offering more flexibility and scalability with lower upfront costs. Both types cater to different preferences and needs of dealers in the automotive industry.</p></p>
