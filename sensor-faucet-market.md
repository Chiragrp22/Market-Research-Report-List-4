<p><p>Sensor faucets are divided into two main types, including infra-red sensor faucets and touch button faucets. Infra-red sensor faucets use infrared technology to detect hand movements and automatically activate the water flow. On the other hand, touch button faucets require physical contact with a button to start the water flow. Both types offer hands-free operation, improved hygiene, and water conservation benefits, making them popular choices in commercial and residential settings.</p></p>
