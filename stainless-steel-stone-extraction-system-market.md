<p><p>Stainless Steel Stone Extraction Systems are available in three main types: 3-Wire, 4-Wire, and 6-Wire designs. In a 3-Wire system, there are three wire ropes connected to the stone extraction basket for lifting and lowering stones. A 4-Wire system has four wire ropes for added stability and control during the extraction process. A 6-Wire system offers even more strength and precision with six wire ropes. Each type caters to different needs and preferences in the stone extraction industry.</p></p>
