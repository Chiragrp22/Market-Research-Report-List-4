<p><p>Electric brake boosters are classified into three main types based on their diaphragm configuration. Single diaphragm boosters use one diaphragm to amplify brake force, making them lightweight and ideal for smaller vehicles. Dual diaphragm boosters incorporate two diaphragms for increased brake power, suitable for larger and heavier vehicles. Other markets may include hybrid diaphragm configurations or proprietary technology that offer unique features such as quicker response times or improved durability. Each type caters to specific vehicle requirements and performance demands in the automotive industry.</p></p>
