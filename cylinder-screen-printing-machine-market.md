<p><p>Cylinder screen printing machines are classified into three main types based on their operation mode: manual, semi-automatic, and fully automatic. Manual machines require operators to manually move the cylinder and load the substrate, making them ideal for small-scale printing. Semi-automatic machines have some automated functions like screen lift and index rotation, providing increased efficiency and productivity. Fully automatic machines are the most advanced, with all functions automated, making them suitable for large-scale production with minimal operator intervention.</p></p>
