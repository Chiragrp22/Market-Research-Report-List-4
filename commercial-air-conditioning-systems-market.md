<p><strong>The Commercial Air Conditioning Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Variable Air Volume (VAV)</li><li>Variable Refrigerant Volume (VRV)</li><li>Fan Control Unit (FCU)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Commercial Air Conditioning Systems market types include Variable Air Volume (VAV), Variable Refrigerant Volume (VRV), and Fan Control Unit (FCU) systems. VAV systems adjust the air flow based on the cooling load, providing energy efficiency. VRV systems control the refrigerant volume to individual units, offering flexibility and precise temperature control. FCU systems use fans to distribute cooled air, providing a simple and cost-effective solution for smaller commercial spaces. Each system type offers unique features to cater to varying commercial air conditioning needs.</p></p>
