<p><p>Elderly nutrition market includes parenteral elderly nutrition, which involves delivering nutrients intravenously, and enteral elderly nutrition, which involves delivering nutrients through a feeding tube. Parenteral elderly nutrition is typically used for individuals who cannot consume food orally, while enteral elderly nutrition is used when individuals have difficulty swallowing or digesting food. Both types of elderly nutrition aim to provide essential nutrients to support the health and well-being of older individuals who may have specific dietary needs or challenges.</p></p>
