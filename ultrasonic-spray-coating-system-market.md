<p><p>Rotary-Type Ultrasonic Spray Coating Systems are characterized by a rotating substrate holder, allowing for uniform coating of cylindrical or spherical objects. On the other hand, Flat-Type Ultrasonic Spray Coating Systems are designed for flat surfaces, providing a consistent and precise coating application. Both types of systems utilize ultrasonic technology to atomize the coating material into fine droplets, resulting in a highly efficient and high-quality coating process. These systems are widely used in various industries such as electronics, automotive, and medical devices for coating applications.</p></p>
