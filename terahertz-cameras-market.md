<p><strong>The Terahertz Cameras Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Passive Terahertz Imaging</li><li>Active Terahertz Imaging</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Terahertz cameras are used for imaging in the terahertz frequency range, which lies between microwave and infrared frequencies. There are two main types of terahertz cameras: passive terahertz imaging and active terahertz imaging. </p><p>Passive terahertz imaging relies on detecting the natural terahertz radiation emitted or reflected by objects, allowing for non-invasive imaging. Active terahertz imaging, on the other hand, involves emitting terahertz radiation and measuring the reflected or scattered signals to create images, enabling better control over the imaging process.</p></p>
