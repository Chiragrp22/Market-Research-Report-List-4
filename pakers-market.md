<p><ul><li>Permanent Packers</li><li>Retrievable Packers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Permanent packers are used in oil and gas wells to isolate different zones and prevent fluids from flowing between them. They are set in place and cannot be moved once installed. Retrievable packers, on the other hand, can be easily removed without damaging the wellbore. They are commonly used in operations where the packer needs to be retrieved and reused or replaced. Both types of packers serve the purpose of isolating zones and controlling fluid flow in wells.</p></p>
