<p><ul><li>Single Phase Cooling</li><li>Two Phase Cooling</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Data Center Liquid Immersion Cooling technology comes in two different types - Single Phase Cooling and Two Phase Cooling. Single Phase Cooling involves immersing servers and other components in a dielectric fluid that remains in liquid state throughout the cooling process. On the other hand, Two Phase Cooling uses a fluid that undergoes a phase change, such as vaporizing, to absorb heat efficiently. Both types offer advantages in terms of cooling efficiency, energy savings, and reduced carbon footprint for data center operations.</p></p>
