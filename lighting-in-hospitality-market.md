<p><p>In the hospitality industry, there are various types of lighting solutions available in the market. CFL (Compact Fluorescent Lamp) is a cost-effective option that provides bright light with low energy consumption. LFL (Linear Fluorescent Lamp) is commonly used for general lighting due to its long lifespan and efficiency. HID (High-Intensity Discharge) lighting offers high output and is often used for outdoor and large space lighting. LED (Light Emitting Diode) lighting is gaining popularity in hospitality as it is energy-efficient, long-lasting, and provides customizable lighting solutions.</p></p>
