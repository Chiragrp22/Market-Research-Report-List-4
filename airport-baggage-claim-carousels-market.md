<p><p>Airport baggage claim carousels come in three main types: flat type, inclined type, and half moon type. The flat type carousel allows luggage to be easily moved on a flat surface, while the inclined type carousel uses a slope to move bags towards passengers. The half moon type carousel is a circular design that can accommodate a large number of bags at once. Each type of carousel offers different advantages and may be preferred based on the specific needs of an airport.</p></p>
