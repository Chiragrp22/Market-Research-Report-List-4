<p><p>Fuel cell DC-DC converters are essential in converting the variable output of fuel cells into stable DC power for various applications. Isolated DC-DC converters provide electrical isolation between input and output, ensuring safety and preventing ground loop issues. On the other hand, non-isolated DC-DC converters are more compact and cost-effective, suitable for applications where isolation is not necessary. Both types cater to different market needs, offering flexibility and reliability in fuel cell power systems.</p></p>
