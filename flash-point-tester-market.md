<p><p>Flash Point Testers are instruments used to determine the lowest temperature at which a liquid produces enough vapor to ignite in the presence of an open flame. There are two main types of Flash Point Testers: Open Cup and Closed Cup testers. Open Cup testers have the sample exposed to air during the test, while Closed Cup testers enclose the sample, creating a more controlled environment. Both types are essential for safety testing in industries such as oil and gas, chemicals, and manufacturing.</p></p>
