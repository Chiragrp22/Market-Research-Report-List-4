<p><strong>The Impingement Syndrome Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Structural Impingement</li><li>Functional Impingement</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Impingement Syndrome Market consists of two main types: Structural Impingement and Functional Impingement. Structural Impingement refers to physical obstructions in the joint such as bone spurs or a misshapen joint that cause friction and pain during movement. Functional Impingement, on the other hand, involves issues with muscle coordination and movement patterns that lead to joint irritation and pain. Both types can result in limited range of motion, discomfort, and potential deterioration of the affected joint if not properly managed.</p></p>
