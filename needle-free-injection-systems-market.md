<p><p>Needle-free injection systems offer various types of technologies for administering drugs without the use of needles. Jet-plane needle-free syringes use high-pressure air to create a thin stream of liquid that penetrates the skin. Spring type needle-free syringes use a powerful spring to deliver the medication. Laser syringes without needles use a laser beam to create a tiny pore in the skin for drug delivery. Vibrating syringes without needles use rapid vibration to propel the medication through the skin. These technologies provide a painless and convenient alternative to traditional injections.</p></p>
