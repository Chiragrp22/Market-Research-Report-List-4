<p><strong>The Organic Food Color Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Water-Soluble</li><li>Oil-Soluble</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Organic food colors are classified into two main types based on their solubility: water-soluble and oil-soluble. Water-soluble organic food colors are mixable with water and are commonly used in products like beverages, icings, and gummies. On the other hand, oil-soluble organic food colors are compatible with oils and fats, making them suitable for products like chocolates, candies, and coatings. Both types offer natural and safe alternatives to synthetic food colors, meeting the growing demand for organic and clean label products.</p></p>
