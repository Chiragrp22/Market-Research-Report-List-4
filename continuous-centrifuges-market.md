<p><p>Continuous centrifuges are classified into two main types based on their operation: Continuous Filtration Centrifuges and Continuous Sedimentation Centrifuges. </p><p>Continuous Filtration Centrifuges operate by continuously feeding a mixture into the centrifuge, which separates the solid particles from the liquid through a filtration process. On the other hand, Continuous Sedimentation Centrifuges function by using centrifugal force to separate particles based on their different densities within the mixture. Both types of centrifuges are essential in various industries for continuous separation processes.</p></p>
