<p><p>Protein chip markets can be categorized into three main types: Reverse Phase Protein Microarray, Functional Protein Microarray, and Analytical Microarray. Reverse Phase Protein Microarray focuses on identifying protein levels in biological samples, while Functional Protein Microarray assesses protein interactions and functions. Analytical Microarray, on the other hand, is used for analyzing protein-protein interactions, protein binding, and protein expression levels. Each type of protein chip market serves a unique purpose in the field of proteomics and biomedical research.</p></p>
