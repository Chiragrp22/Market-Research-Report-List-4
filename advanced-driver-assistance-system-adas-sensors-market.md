Camera Sensors use image recognition technology to identify road signs, pedestrians, and other vehicles. Radar Sensors use radio waves to detect the presence of objects and determine their distance and speed. Lidar Sensors use laser pulses to create detailed 3D maps of the surroundings. Each type of sensor plays a crucial role in enhancing the safety and efficiency of the vehicle by providing important data to the ADAS system.</p></p>
