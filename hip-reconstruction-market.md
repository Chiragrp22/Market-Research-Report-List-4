<p><p>Hip reconstruction market includes several types of procedures such as primary cemented hip replacement, primary cementless hip replacement, partial hip replacement, revision hip replacement, and hip resurfacing. Primary cemented hip replacement involves using cement to attach the prosthesis to the bone, while primary cementless hip replacement does not require cement for attachment. Partial hip replacement involves replacing only part of the hip joint. Revision hip replacement is performed to replace a previous hip replacement that has failed. Hip resurfacing involves placing a metal cap over the femoral head instead of completely replacing the joint.</p></p>
