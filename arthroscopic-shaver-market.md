<p><p>An arthroscopic shaver is a medical device used in arthroscopic surgery to remove damaged tissue, cartilage, or bone from a joint. It consists of a small, rotating blade on the end of a shaft that is inserted into the joint through a small incision. The shaver is controlled by the surgeon and allows for precise removal of unwanted tissue without causing unnecessary trauma to surrounding healthy tissue.</p><p>The Arthroscopic Shaver Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 13.7% during the forecast period.
