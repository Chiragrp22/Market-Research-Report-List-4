<p><p>Distance measurement systems can be categorized into three main types: optical, laser, and others. Optical systems use cameras or sensors to measure distances by analyzing the captured images. Laser systems use laser beams to accurately measure distances by calculating the time taken for the laser beam to reflect back. Other systems may include ultrasound or radar technology for distance measurement. Each type of system has its own advantages and applications based on factors like accuracy, range, and environment.</p></p>
