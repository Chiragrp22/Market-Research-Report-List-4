<p><p>Radiation Detection Products market can be categorized into Dose Detection and Non-dose Detection. Dose Detection products are designed to measure and monitor the amount of radiation exposure, such as dosimeters used by healthcare workers and radiology technicians. On the other hand, Non-dose Detection products are used for identifying the presence of radiation without measuring the exact dose, such as radiation detectors used for security purposes in airports. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring safety and preventing potential risks associated with radiation exposure.</p></p>
