<p><p>Suture wire market types can be categorized into absorbable and non-absorbable suture wires. Absorbable suture wires are designed to break down and be absorbed by the body over time, reducing the need for removal. On the other hand, non-absorbable suture wires are not intended to be broken down by the body and may need to be removed after the wound has healed. Both types serve different purposes and are used based on the specific needs of the patient and the nature of the injury.</p></p>
