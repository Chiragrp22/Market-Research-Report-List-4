<p><p>Zero pressure accumulation conveyor systems are available in two main types: photo eye sensors and roller adjustment market. Photo eye sensors utilize sensors to detect the presence of products on the conveyor, ensuring that items are evenly spaced and preventing collisions. In comparison, roller adjustment market systems use strategically placed rollers to control product flow and accumulation. Both types of systems help improve efficiency, reduce product damage, and ensure smooth operation in warehouses and distribution centers.</p></p>
