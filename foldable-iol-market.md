<p><strong>The Foldable IOL Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Refractive Type</li><li>Diffraction Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Foldable IOL market is segmented into two main types: refractive type and diffraction type. </p><p>Refractive type IOLs use the principles of refraction to correct vision problems, such as nearsightedness, farsightedness, and astigmatism. These IOLs are designed to provide clear vision at various distances.</p><p>On the other hand, diffraction type IOLs work by diffracting light into multiple focal points, allowing for improved vision at various distances. These IOLs are commonly used for correcting presbyopia, a condition that affects near vision in aging individuals.</p></p>
