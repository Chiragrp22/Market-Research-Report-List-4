<p><p>X-ray detectors encompass various types, including amorphous silicon and amorphous selenium (TFT) detectors, CMOS detectors, and others. Amorphous silicon and amorphous selenium (TFT) detectors utilize thin film transistor technology for high-resolution imaging. CMOS detectors, on the other hand, offer lower noise and higher dynamic range, making them suitable for a wide range of applications. Other types of X-ray detectors may include direct and indirect conversion detectors, each with unique advantages and applications in medical imaging and industrial inspection.</p></p>
