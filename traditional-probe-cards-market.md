<p><p>Traditional Probe Cards market consists of two main types - Blade/Tungsten Probe Cards and Epoxy/Cantilever Probe Cards. Blade/Tungsten Probe Cards use sharp blades or tungsten needles to make contact with the semiconductor wafer, providing high accuracy and precision. Epoxy/Cantilever Probe Cards use epoxy or cantilever technology for contacting the wafer, offering higher flexibility and longer lifespan. Both types are widely used in the semiconductor industry for testing and inspecting wafers during the manufacturing process.</p></p>
