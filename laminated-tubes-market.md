<p><p>Laminated tubes are widely used for packaging in various industries. The two main types of laminated tubes in the market are Plastic Barrier Laminate (PBL) and Aluminium Barrier Laminate (ABL). PBL tubes are made of different layers of plastic, providing excellent barrier properties against moisture and air. ABL tubes, on the other hand, have a layer of aluminum in between the plastic layers, offering superior barrier protection against light, oxygen, and moisture. Both types of laminated tubes are versatile and effective for packaging various products.</p></p>
