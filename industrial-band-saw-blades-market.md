<p><p>Industrial band saw blades come in two main types - bimetal and carbide tipped. Bimetal blades are made from two different metals, typically high-speed steel and carbon steel, which are welded together. These blades offer a good balance of cutting performance and durability. On the other hand, carbide tipped blades feature carbide teeth that are brazed onto the blade, providing exceptional cutting performance and longer blade life. Each type has its own advantages and is suited for different cutting applications in the industrial sector.</p></p>
