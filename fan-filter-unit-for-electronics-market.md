<p><p>A Fan Filter Unit (FFU) is used in electronics to efficiently filter and remove contaminants from the air. There are two main types in the market: HEPA FFU and ULPA FFU. HEPA FFUs are designed to capture particles as small as 0.3 microns, making them suitable for most cleanroom environments. ULPA FFUs have an even higher filtration efficiency, capturing particles as small as 0.1 microns, making them ideal for sensitive electronics manufacturing processes that require ultra-clean air.</p></p>
