<p><p>Sand control equipment market can be categorized into different types based on their functionality. Gravel pack equipment is used to control sand production by injecting gravel into the reservoir. Fracturing pack equipment is used for hydraulic fracturing to stimulate oil and gas production while controlling sand influx. Sand control screens are designed to filter out sand particles while allowing fluid flow. Other types of equipment in the market include slotted liners, standalone screens, and chemical injection systems, all aimed at effectively managing sand production in oil and gas wells.</p></p>
