<p><p>Wheel speed sensors are a crucial component in the automotive industry, particularly in anti-lock braking systems (ABS). There are two main types of wheel speed sensors: Hall type and magnetic electric type. Hall type sensors use a Hall effect semiconductor to measure the rotation of the wheel, while magnetic electric type sensors utilize magnets to detect wheel speed. Both types are essential for ensuring the proper functioning of ABS systems, providing accurate data to control braking and prevent skidding. The market for wheel speed sensors continues to grow as demand for vehicle safety features increases.</p></p>
