Short-Acting Bronchodilators provide quick relief by relaxing the muscles around the airways. Corticosteroids reduce inflammation in the airways. Methylxanthines help to relax the muscles in the lungs. Long-Acting Bronchodilators provide long-term relief by relaxing the airway muscles. Combination Drugs combine two different types of medications to provide better control of COPD symptoms.</p></p>
