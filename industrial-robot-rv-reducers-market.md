<p><p>Industrial robot RV reducers come in two main types: Flange output type and hollow type. Flange output type reducers have a solid output shaft with a flange for easy mounting connections. They are commonly used in applications requiring high precision and stiffness. On the other hand, hollow type reducers have a hollow output shaft, allowing for easy integration of cables, hoses, or other components. They are often used in applications where space-saving and weight reduction are important factors. Both types play a crucial role in enhancing the efficiency and performance of industrial robots.</p></p>
