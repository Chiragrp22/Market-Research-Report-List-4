<p><p>5G network infrastructure market includes various types such as Femto Cell, Pico Cell, Micro Cell, and Macro Cell. Femto Cell is designed for residential or small business environments, providing low-power wireless coverage over a small area. Pico Cell covers slightly larger areas, typically used indoors in areas such as offices or shopping malls. Micro Cell offers more coverage and capacity compared to Pico Cell, suitable for urban areas. Macro Cell is used to cover large areas such as cities or rural areas, providing wide coverage and high capacity.</p></p>
